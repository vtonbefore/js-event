About Adele

Adele Laurie Blue Adkins MBE (born 5 May 1988) is an English singer-songwriter. After graduating from the BRIT School in 2006, Adele signed a recording contract with XL Recordings.

Her debut album, 19, was released in 2008 to commercial and critical success. Her second album, 21, earned her widespread recognition and numerous awards. Her third album, 25, became the best-selling album of 2015 and 2016.
